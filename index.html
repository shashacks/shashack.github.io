About me

My name is Ju-Seung Byun. I am a third-year computer science & enginnering Ph.D. student at The Ohio State University advised by Andrew Perrault. My research interests are in deep reinforcement learning, especially focusing on hierarchical structure and distributional refinforcement learning.

Before I came to OSU, I received MY Master’s degree from University of Southern California (2019) and my Bachelor’s degree from Inha University (2017) in South Korea, both in Computer Science.
